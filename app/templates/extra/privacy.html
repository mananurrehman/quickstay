1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using QuickStay:

Personal Information

First name, last name, username, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.

Booking Information

Check-in and check-out dates, number of guests, room preferences, and booking history.

Communication Data

Messages sent through our contact form, including name, email, subject, and message content.

Usage Data

Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To process and manage your room bookings
  • To send booking confirmations and cancellation notifications via email
  • To facilitate password recovery through OTP verification
  • To respond to your inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

Password Encryption

All passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. We never store plain-text passwords.

CSRF Protection

All forms are protected against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks using secure tokens.

SQL Injection Prevention

We use parameterized queries and ORM to prevent SQL injection attacks.

Session Security

Sessions automatically timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity for your protection.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your login session and authentication state.
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your theme preference (dark/light mode).
  • Security Cookies: To protect against CSRF attacks and ensure secure form submissions.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Email Service: For sending booking confirmations, password reset OTPs, and notifications.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View your personal information through your profile page.
  • Update: Edit your profile information including name, email, phone, and password.
  • Export: Request a copy of your personal data and booking history.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting our support team.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Account information is retained until you request deletion.
  • Booking records are maintained for historical and administrative purposes.
  • Password reset OTPs are automatically deleted after 10 minutes.
